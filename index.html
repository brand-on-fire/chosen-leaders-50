Welcome!

Charisse R. Drakeford, a native of Chicago, Illinois is a prolific Bible study teacher and author. Charisse currently resides in China. She focuses on training leadership in worship and arts and other ministries. She admonishes all the ministry leaders to offer their gifts back to the Lord.

Charisse stresses in her book, “Who Are You Listening to? Developing the Leader in You,” to surrender before the Lord and allow the Holy Spirit to move. Charisse is a devoted mother to Mya’, godmother and god-grandmother to Renita and A’riyah Godbold.

Charisse is a dedicated leader in The Eagles Network (TEN),Civitan International Club, and Clarkston First Baptist Church where she led in the dance and prison ministry. For the next step in listening to the voice of God, obtain her journal, “Write the Vision: A 50 Day Journey.”

About

Charisse R. Drakeford is a prolific Bible teacher. She is passionate about teaching leaders in the worship arts and other ministries. Charisse is a leader in her church, as well as several local and international clubs.
